## FAQ

**Can I try paid features before upgrading?**
Free users see blurred previews of trend charts, activity feeds, and AI insights. You can see that the features exist and what they look like — then upgrade when you're ready.

**How does seat-based pricing work?**
The Team plan has a $25/month base fee plus $5/month per team member. The org owner counts as the first seat.

**Can I downgrade?**
Yes. Manage your subscription from Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your billing period.

**Is the Action free?**
Always. The GitHub Action is open source and runs in your CI using your compute. The cloud dashboard is the paid product.
